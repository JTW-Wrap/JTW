Wrap & Protection

BODY PROTECT

Invisible Protection: We use premium TPU material to keep your vehicle’s original paint safe for a long time.

Self-Healing Technology: Minor scratches repair themselves with heat.

Resistant to External Factors: Durable against bird droppings, light scratches, and other elements.

Hydrophobic Surface: Repels water and dirt for a cleaner finish.

Glossy and Matte Options: Glossy film adds up to 30% extra shine, while matte offers a sleek, sophisticated look.

Window Protection

Privacy & Style: Dark tints offer a sleek look while protecting your privacy inside the car.

UV & Heat Blocking: Rejects harmful UV rays and reduces interior heat for a cooler ride.

Glare Reduction: Improves visibility by cutting sun glare and headlight dazzle.

Legal Compliance: Available in tint levels that comply with Japan's road safety laws.

Light Protection

Clear Lens Guard: Prevents yellowing and cracking of headlights and tail lamps.

Impact Resistance: Minimizes damage from gravel, insects, and weather elements.

Stylish Tint Option: Optional smoke or black-out films for a sleek custom look.

Easy Maintenance: Film is easy to clean and maintain, keeping lights clear.

Color Wrap Film

Choose Your Color, Create Your Style: We offer high-performance films like TPU, CPU, and PET from internationally recognized brands for your color change wraps.

TPU Material: Premium quality with lightweight self-healing properties to keep your vehicle looking brand new.

CPU Material: Durable and budget-friendly, making it a favorite among our customers.

PET Material: Ideal for those seeking economical and basic protection.

Personalized Solutions: Choose your preferred brand and material freely; we provide custom-tailored wrap solutions just for you.
